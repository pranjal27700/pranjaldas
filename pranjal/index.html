Da Vinci Self-Supported Bridge

📍 Barisal Engineering College

A remarkable structural experiment based on Leonardo da Vinci's design for a self-supporting bridge. Constructed at Barisal Engineering College, this bridge utilizes friction and gravity alone to maintain its structural integrity without the need for fasteners or glue. It demonstrates the perfect harmony between engineering mechanics and architectural beauty.
